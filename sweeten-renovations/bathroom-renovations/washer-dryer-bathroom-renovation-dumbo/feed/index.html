A select number will allow it with the proper approvals and permits. “If your building doesn’t allow the installation of a washer and dryer, don’t take it personally,” said Sweeten contractor Majid. “There is a logical reason why. Should your unapproved appliances malfunction and damage units adjacent to or beneath yours, you’ll be responsible.
